About

I'm a Data Science MSc candidate at the University of York with a Distinction BEng in Computer Science from Atria University, Bangalore. My work sits at the crossroads of machine learning, large language models, and responsible AI development.

My dissertation focuses on enabling natural language querying of structured relational databases through LLMs and RAG pipelines — with a strong emphasis on hallucination reduction, safety alignment, and production-grade reliability.

I'm drawn to projects where data tells a meaningful story — from analysing racism trends during COVID-19 to predicting hospital bed occupancy. I care as much about governance and ethics as I do about model performance.
